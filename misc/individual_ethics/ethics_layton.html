AI in the workforce.

Ethics

One of the greatest concerns people have with the evolution of AI is job security. Once a hunk of metal is designed to do your job with no mistakes and by a flick of a switch you would be worried too. Many machines in a business sense can be seen as a one-off purchase to help complete jobs more efficiently than most humans may do. However, in a world where more and more people are finding themselves unemployed it wouldnâ€™t be fair to replace all these people with machines. We believe that if AI were to be introduced into a workplace, it should be assigned jobs that would be deemed unsafe or harmful for a human to complete. This achieves both keeping everyone their job and dealing with certain tasks that humans may not be able to complete.
